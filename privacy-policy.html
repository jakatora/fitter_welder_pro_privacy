2. Scope

This policy explains what information may be processed when you use Fitter Welder Pro and how it is stored.

Fitter Welder Pro is a tool for:

  • creating and managing projects and cut lists,
  • storing welding parameters / settings,
  • working locally on your device (Windows/Android/iOS).

3. Information we process

3.1 User-provided content

The app stores information you enter, such as project/segment/item names, dimensions, materials, angles, welding parameters, and notes.

This content may contain personal data only if you include it yourself (for example names in a project title).

3.2 Files you choose

If you use import/export or pick a file, the app may access files you explicitly select through the system file picker. The app does not scan your device in the background.

3.3 Technical data

In the default configuration, the app does not collect marketing analytics, does not send telemetry to third-party analytics services, and does not create cloud user accounts.

Your operating system or app store may still process limited technical data for installation/updates.

4. Device permissions

The app generally does not require sensitive permissions (such as location, contacts, or microphone). If future versions introduce features that require permissions, we will update this policy and request your consent as required by the platform.

5. Where data is stored

App data is stored locally on your device using an on-device database (SQLite) and/or app files.

6. Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

We may disclose data only when you export/share it yourself, or when required by law.

7. Retention and deletion

Local data remains on your device until you delete it. You can delete data by deleting projects/records in the app (if available), uninstalling the app (typically removes app data), and deleting any exported files you created.

8. Security

We use reasonable measures within the app (local storage). Security also depends on your device protections (device password, disk encryption, backups).

9. Your rights

To the extent we process personal data, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing. Because data is typically stored locally, exercising these rights usually means editing/deleting data in the app or on your device.

10. Children’s privacy

The app is not directed to children and is not intended to collect children’s data.

11. Changes

We may update this policy (for example if online features are added). Updates will be published with app updates and/or on this page.

12. Contact

For privacy questions: [support email].
